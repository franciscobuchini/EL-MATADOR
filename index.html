¡HOLA!


We have been serving Wellington's best Spanish tapas and authentic Argentinian steaks since 2012.

Boasting NZ's first wood-fired asadores and parrilla, an open kitchen, sunny courtyard, boutique bar, superb service and a great vibe, El Matador is the perfect place for a delicious dinner.

Please just come on in!

El Matador is a "walk-in" (no reservations) restaurant.

If a table isn't immediately available when you arrive, you are welcome to wait at our bar or in our NEW courtyard.

OUR TEAM



Mike Marsland

Owner


Mike Marsland is an award winning restaurateur and fully qualified chef in French cuisine since 1980.

Mike has a long history in Wellington’s restaurant scene as the owner of classics like ERNESTO and more recently EKIM BURGERS among others.



Braeden Hindley

Head Chef


Braeden has worked in several restaurants, including other BBQ places which gave him the experience to take over the head chef position.

He brought fresh ideas and beautiful creations for our menu which set a new standard for the restaurant.



Guillermo Domber

General Manager


Guillermo or “G” as everyone knows him has worked at El Matador almost since the beginning which gives him an understanding of the restaurant like no one else.

Guille and his staff helped to make El Matador the success that is today.
